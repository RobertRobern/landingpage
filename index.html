Types of Bikes

Road bicycles are defined by their thin tires and drop handlebars that allow the rider to get in a more aerodynamic position ideal for high-speed racing.

Mountain biking typically come with just a front suspension fork (hardtails) or with both front and rear suspension, more known as full-suspension mountain bikes.

Hybrid bikes are mountain bike with the reduced weight and additional speed of a road bike with stronger alloy frames, upright geometry, and straight handlebars.

City Bikes are the go-to choice for those who simply need a bike to get from A to B with not too much of a hustle. Often the cheapest selection compared to other bike types.
