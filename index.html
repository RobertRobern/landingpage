Types of Bikes

Road bicycles defined by their slender tires and tumble handles that allow a rider to enter aerodynamic space suitable for high-speed racing.

Mountain biking it usually comes with a front suspension fork (stiff tails) or with a front and rear suspension, best known for fully mounted mountain bikes.

Hybrid bikes mountain bikes with reduced weight and extra speed bike road with solid alloy frames, straight geometry, and straight handles.

City Bikes they are the preferred choice for those who need a bike to get from point A to B without too much traffic. It is usually a cheaper choice compared to other types of bikes.
